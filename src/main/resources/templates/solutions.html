3QScan

Contactless, accurate attendance solution with geofencing & liveness detection

FaceMatch is an AI-powered facial recognition platform designed to simplify attendance tracking, access control, and identity verification across educational institutions, corporate offices, retail environments, and event venues.

With advanced facial recognition, geo-fencing, and liveness detection, FaceMatch enables organizations to eliminate manual attendance processes, improve security, and streamline operations through a contactless and highly accurate verification experience.

Why FaceMatch?

📋

Contactless Attendance Management

Enable employees, students, visitors, and event attendees to mark attendance effortlessly using facial recognition technology.

🔐

AI-Powered Identity Verification

Verify identities in real time with high accuracy while reducing the risk of proxy attendance and unauthorized access.

📍

Geo-Fencing & Location Validation

Ensure attendance and verification are performed only from authorized locations.

🎯

Liveness Detection

Prevent spoofing attempts through photos or videos using advanced liveness validation.

📊

Real-Time Monitoring

Track attendance, access events, and verification activities through a centralized cloud-based administration portal.

🔗

Easy Integration

Integrate FaceMatch seamlessly with existing HRMS, ERP, Student Information Systems, Visitor Management Systems, and Access Control Platforms through simple API connectivity.
